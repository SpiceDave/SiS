Sponsorship & Partners

Science in Sport has a unique heritage in working with the world’s top athletes and teams. It is these relationships that enable us to constantly learn and understand what it takes to achieve the ultimate performance out of the world’s very best sportsmen and women. This understanding is applied to every single product in the SiS range, therefore no matter what level you perform at you can be confident you are using product fit for a pro.

We work with an unprecedented number of pro cycling teams, making us the most used sports nutrition brand at this years Tour De France and Tour of Britain.

Sir Chris Hoy

Sir Chris Hoy is a six-time Olympic track cycling gold medalist and Britain’s most successful Olympian.

Helen Jenkins

Helen Jenkins is Great Britain’s leading female triathlete who has twice been an ITU World Champion.

Belkin ProCycling

Previously known as the Blanco Cycling Team, the newly formed Belkin Pro Cycling Team will again be fuelled by SiS during 2013.

Rapha Condor JLT

SiS is the exclusive sports nutritional supplier to Rapha Condor JLT in 2013, a leading British UCI Continental professional cycling team racing in events both in the UK and Europe.

Katusha

This Russian Super-Team represents the Russian Global Cycling Project and will be fuelled by SiS in 2013.
